WHO WE ARE

Fiori Concrete Machines India Pvt. Ltd., a wholly owned subsidiary of Fiori Group S.p.A. Italy was registered in India in March 2022. The principal company, Fiori Group S.p.A., was founded in 1942 & is headquartered in Finale Emilia (MO) in Italy.


The Fiori factory set up in Chennai, India, is the only factory outside of Italy for manufacturing Fiori self-loading mixers. The Fiori self-loading mixers are a result of continuous innovation over the last 80 years, extensive R&D, and customer feedback from over 65 countries.

we provide the best quality concrete machines

India is not new to Fiori & neither is Fiori new to India. Fiori machines have been successfully installed & working across India for more than 25 years now.

We are eager to directly serve our customers in India & deliver the best possible technology & knowledge. We request you to contact us for any queries.
